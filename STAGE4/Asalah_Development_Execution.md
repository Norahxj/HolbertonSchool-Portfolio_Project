# Stage 4 – MVP Development and Execution

## Purpose

This stage focused on implementing the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) using Agile principles. The project was organized into four one-week sprints to support iterative development, continuous integration, quality assurance, and regular team collaboration.

# Development Summary

The MVP was developed over four one-week sprints. Throughout development, the team followed Agile practices including sprint planning, daily collaboration, continuous testing, code reviews, bug tracking, and sprint reviews. Quality assurance activities were integrated into every sprint to ensure that new features met the required quality standards before deployment.
